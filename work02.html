CANADA JOURNAL

DESIGN / DEVELOPMENT

Visit

OVERVIEW

Canadian Journal offers services for foreign students to study abroad. Mainly in order for international students, conducting local school procedures, counseling, and visa application procedures.
Since the former site information is outdated and the correspondence of the mobile site is not done firmly, I had created a new site this time.

THE PREVIOUS VERSION

Since there was not text size rule and was too much information on the whole site, It made it hard to find the information the user wanted to see. Also, the mobile site was not well made.

IMAGE MOVIE

I also had created an image video of this service. I primarily used an Adobe After Effect and Premiere for this video.

WEB SITE

In order to solve the problem which the former site had, I contacted the client as much as possible until each other could be satisfied and worked forward.
I and client tried to think to cut some information and trying to create a simple and easy-to-understand design so that user can understand the service content as soon as possible.
